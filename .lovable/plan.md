

# Loy Krathong Night — Event Landing Page

## Overview
A cinematic, high-conversion landing page for a romantic floating-lights evening on a German ship restaurant. The design will feel like a luxury event campaign — poetic, immersive, and built to capture leads.

## Color & Design System
- **Background**: Deep navy (`hsl(220, 40%, 8%)`) with dark gradients
- **Gold accent**: Warm amber/gold for CTAs, highlights, icons
- **Cream**: Elegant light text on dark backgrounds
- **Typography**: Large, editorial headline hierarchy with serif-inspired feel using elegant Google Fonts (Playfair Display for headlines, Inter for body)
- **Mood**: Candlelit, cinematic, intimate

## Page Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-viewport cinematic hero with dark gradient overlay
- Poetic headline: *"A Night of Floating Lights"*
- Emotional subheadline about candlelit riverside celebration
- Date/location placeholders styled as elegant badges
- Two CTAs: "Reserve Your Place" (gold) + "Join Early Access" (outline)
- Subtle floating/glow animation on decorative elements

### 2. "The Experience" Section
- Storytelling layout with staggered text blocks and large placeholder image areas
- Describes the sensory journey: candles, floating floral lights, Thai-inspired atmosphere, soft music, intimacy
- Limited capacity callout woven into narrative
- Warm amber accent lines/dividers

### 3. Visual Highlights (4 Cards)
- Elegant cards with placeholder image areas and overlay text:
  1. Floating Candlelight Atmosphere
  2. Thai-Inspired Culinary Journey
  3. Romantic Riverfront Setting
  4. Limited Premium Seating
- Each with a short evocative paragraph

### 4. "Why This Night Is Special" Section
- Icon-led grid or elegant list with gold accents
- Points: rare venue, one-of-a-kind concept, shareable memories, ideal for couples & special occasions
- Exclusivity and scarcity language

### 5. Event Details Section
- Structured info cards in a clean grid: Date, Time, Boarding, Capacity, Pricing, Menu, Weather, Reservation Policy
- Dark card style with gold icon accents

### 6. Reservation CTA Block
- Mid-page conversion section with warm gradient background
- Headline: "Reserve Your Place Before Public Release"
- Email input + submit button
- Lead magnet: "20% off first event" or "3 tickets for price of 2"
- Scarcity badges: "Limited tickets", "Early-access priority"
- Feels like joining an exclusive community

### 7. Social/Content Moment Section
- Elegant section about the event being visually documented
- Placeholder image mosaic/grid suggesting premium event photography
- Copy about creating beautiful, shareable memories

### 8. FAQ Section
- Accordion-style with gold accents
- 6 questions covering authenticity, dinner inclusion, atmosphere, dress code, groups, weather

### 9. Final CTA Footer
- Full-width section with dark cinematic background
- Final emotional headline: *"Some Nights Stay With You Forever"*
- Email capture form + reserve button
- Limited places reminder
- Minimal footer with brand mark

## Technical Approach
- Single-page React app with smooth scroll sections
- Responsive design (mobile-first)
- CSS animations for subtle glow/fade effects
- Lead capture form with toast confirmation
- All placeholder images structured for easy replacement later
